### Attributes (Derived from Business + IKIGAI + Stories)

- **Authoritative:** rooted in deep expertise and proven outcomes
- **Empathetic:** shaped by global upbringing and lived adversity
- **Practical:** systems and frameworks that actually work
- **Strategic:** always connecting actions to revenue impact
- **Calm & Grounded:** influenced by your personal discipline, prayer, fitness, and global worldview

### Language to Use

- "Revenue engine"
- "Alignment"
- "Predictability"
- "Outcome-driven"
- "Operating model"
- "Value realization"
