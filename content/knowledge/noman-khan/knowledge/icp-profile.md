## ICP Persona Summary (~150 words)

CXO Collective serves growth-to-enterprise SaaS and cybersecurity leaders responsible for revenue retention and customer outcomes. The ideal buyer is a CRO, CCO, or VP Customer Success operating within a 50–2,000 employee business generating $10M–$500M ARR and facing churn pressure, slow adoption cycles, or complexity from scaling internationally. These leaders know revenue leakage exists but lack system-wide clarity across sales, product, and post-sales. They seek measurable NRR gains, predictable renewals, and operational control—not surface tools or temporary fixes. Emotionally, they are driven by accountability, reputation among peers, and the desire to build a durable customer engine that proves strategic leadership. Their purchase decisions accelerate during growth inflection points when internal attempts stall and leadership alignment demands a proven operator's framework to stabilize revenue while preparing the organization for global scale.

## Strategic Content Direction (~100 words)

Content should educate executive buyers on diagnosing systemic churn, aligning GTM motions, and turning customer success into a growth engine. Focus on leadership perspective rather than tooling advice—case-style breakdowns, transformation playbooks, and real-world operating insights resonate most. Use narratives centered on "from chaos to control," emphasizing measurable outcomes like improved NRR, faster time-to-value, and predictable expansion. Address emotional themes: credibility as a revenue leader, peace of mind around forecasting, and pride in building elite CS organizations. Position CXO Collective as the bridge between boardroom growth goals and post-sales execution that actually delivers.
