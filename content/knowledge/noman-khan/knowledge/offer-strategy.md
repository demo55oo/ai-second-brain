# Noman Khan — Offer Strategy, Pricing & Monetization Blueprint

**Prepared for the 2-Day Strategy Session**
**December 2025**

## Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive market research of the Customer Success consulting landscape and deep analysis of Noman's documented expertise, this document outlines two high-credibility offers designed to generate revenue within the next 30–60 days.

### The Core Monetization Problem

Noman's current content generates engagement but fails to convert because:

- No packaged offer exists that buyers can say "yes" to
- Generic leadership content attracts peers, not buyers with budgets
- Zero commercial intent in content (no bridge from insight to action)
- Positioning confusion between "consultant" and "job seeker"

### The Recommended Solution

Create a two-tier offer structure with a high-ticket core offer and a low-friction entry point that attracts qualified leads:

- **PRIMARY OFFER:** CS Operating Model Diagnostic ($25K–$50K)
- **ENTRY POINT:** 30-Minute Revenue Leak Assessment (Free → Paid Consult)

## Part 1: Competitive Landscape Analysis

The Customer Success consulting market is growing rapidly, with several established players and emerging fractional leadership models. Understanding how competitors structure their offers reveals opportunities for differentiation.

### Key Competitors & Their Offers

#### 1. The Success League
**Focus:** CS team training and certification

- Offers modular consulting engagements (2 days to 1 year)
- CSM Certification Programs (15-week courses)
- CS Leadership Development programs
- Weekly coaching calls for DIY leaders

**Gap:** Training-focused, not operating model transformation. Doesn't address systemic fragmentation.

#### 2. CSM Practice
**Focus:** Customer success strategy consulting (since 2014)

- Customer Success strategy design & implementation
- QBR and renewal forecast optimization
- Success Plan process redesign
- Partners with over 100 mid-market tech companies

**Gap:** CS-department focused. Doesn't address product-sales-services alignment or global operating model.

#### 3. Growth Molecules
**Focus:** AI-driven CS strategies and tech implementation

- Diagnostic assessments with benchmarks
- Fractional leadership (CCO, VP CS)
- CSP implementation (Gainsight, Planhat, etc.)
- Operational playbooks and training workshops

**Gap:** Technology-implementation heavy. Limited focus on commercial transformation or cybersecurity expertise.

#### 4. Gainsight Strategic Advisory Services (GSAS)
**Focus:** CS strategy tied to Gainsight platform adoption

- One-day leadership workshops
- Custom-scoped consulting projects
- Operationalizing customer lifecycle

**Gap:** Tied to their software platform. Limited independence and conflicts of interest.

#### 5. Fractional CCO Market
**Focus:** Part-time executive leadership (1–3 days/week)

- Chameleon Collective, FRAKXION, and independents
- Typical engagement: 3-month to 12-month retainer
- Pricing: $10K–$25K/month depending on days/week

**Gap:** Ongoing operational role, not transformation-focused. Harder to sell without existing relationship.

### Noman's Competitive Differentiation

Based on the competitive landscape, Noman has three unique differentiators that no competitor currently claims:

**Differentiator 1: Full Lifecycle Operating Model**
Competitors focus on CS-only solutions. Noman addresses the entire product → sales → services → CS alignment problem. This is the root cause of churn, not symptoms.

**Differentiator 2: Cybersecurity Industry Expertise**
No major CS consulting firm has deep cybersecurity vertical expertise combined with CS operating model experience. Noman has led transformation at Imperva and built global cyber practices.

**Differentiator 3: Strategy + Operational Execution**
Most consultants deliver PowerPoint strategies. Noman has personally built and scaled $35M–$65M+ post-sales organizations with documented results (+168% ACV, +8% GRR).

## Part 2: Offer Architecture

The following offer structure is designed based on Noman's documented methodology (8-week diagnostic) and proven commercial outcomes.

### PRIMARY OFFER: The Revenue Alignment Diagnostic

> "Transform fragmented customer operations into a predictable revenue engine in 8 weeks."

#### Offer Summary

- **Duration:** 8 weeks
- **Price Range:** $35,000 – $50,000 (based on company size and complexity)
- **Target Buyer:** CRO, CCO, VP Customer Success, CEO at SaaS/Cybersecurity companies ($10M–$500M ARR)
- **Urgency Trigger:** Rising churn, NRR below 100%, PE/Board pressure, post-acquisition chaos

#### What's Included

**Phase 1: Discovery & Assessment (Weeks 1–2)**

- Executive stakeholder interviews (CRO, CCO, Product, Sales, Services leaders)
- Customer lifecycle mapping across all touchpoints
- Current-state operating model documentation
- Data review: NRR, GRR, churn patterns, time-to-value metrics

**Phase 2: Gap Analysis & Root Cause (Weeks 3–4)**

- Revenue leakage identification across the lifecycle
- Cross-functional alignment assessment (Product-Sales-Services-CS)
- Onboarding friction points and time-to-value blockers
- Governance and workflow inefficiencies

**Phase 3: Operating Model Design (Weeks 5–6)**

- Future-state operating model architecture
- Customer value definition and outcome frameworks
- Team structure and leadership alignment recommendations
- KPI governance tied to NRR, GRR, and expansion

**Phase 4: Roadmap & Implementation Plan (Weeks 7–8)**

- 90-day, 6-month, and 12-month implementation roadmap
- Quick wins identification (immediate impact opportunities)
- Executive readout presentation to leadership team
- Playbook templates for onboarding, adoption, and renewal

#### Deliverables

- Customer Lifecycle Diagnostic Report (40–60 pages)
- Revenue-Aligned Operating Model Blueprint
- Implementation Roadmap with Milestones
- Executive Summary Presentation
- Quick Wins Playbook (3–5 immediate action items)
- 30-day post-engagement follow-up call

#### Pricing Rationale

At $35K–$50K, this positions Noman in the premium consulting tier while remaining accessible to growth-stage companies. Using value-based pricing logic: if the engagement prevents just 2–3% additional churn or accelerates NRR by 3–5 points on a $10M ARR business, the ROI is 5–10x.

### Tiered Pricing Options

Offering three pricing tiers gives buyers choice and anchors the mid-tier as the "smart choice":

| Tier | Price | What's Included | Best For |
|------|-------|-----------------|----------|
| **Option 1: Essentials** | $25,000 | 4-week compressed diagnostic; Executive stakeholder interviews (3–5 leaders); Gap analysis and root cause identification; Executive summary report with top 5 recommendations | Companies needing validation before larger investment |
| **Option 2: Transformation** *(RECOMMENDED)* | $40,000 | Full 8-week diagnostic as described above; All deliverables included; Two executive workshop sessions; 30-day post-engagement support call | Companies ready to redesign their operating model |
| **Option 3: Acceleration** | $65,000 | Everything in Option 2; 90-day implementation support (4 hours/week advisory); Quarterly leadership review sessions (3 sessions); Priority access to Noman for escalation calls | PE-backed companies under pressure to show rapid progress |

### LOW-FRICTION ENTRY POINT: The Revenue Leak Assessment

> "Discover where your customer lifecycle is leaking revenue — in 30 minutes."

This is the lead generation mechanism that converts engaged LinkedIn followers into sales conversations.

#### How It Works

**Step 1: Content CTA**
At the end of problem-aware posts, include: *"DM me 'REVENUE' for a free 30-minute Revenue Leak Assessment."*

**Step 2: Qualification DM**
When someone DMs, send a brief qualifying message:

> "Thanks for reaching out. Before we schedule, can you tell me: 1) What's your current NRR? 2) What's your biggest post-sales challenge right now? 3) What's your ARR range?"

**Step 3: The Assessment Call**

- 30-minute Zoom call
- Ask diagnostic questions about their customer lifecycle
- Identify 2–3 obvious gaps or misalignments
- Offer immediate value by naming the root cause
- Bridge to the paid engagement: *"What I've shared is the tip of the iceberg. My full diagnostic goes 10x deeper."*

**Step 4: Proposal**
Send a tailored proposal within 48 hours referencing specific issues discussed on the call.

#### Alternative Entry: Paid Strategy Session

For leads who aren't ready for the full diagnostic but need immediate guidance: offer a 2-hour paid strategy session at $1,500–$2,500. This qualifies serious buyers and creates a natural upsell path.

## Part 3: Content-to-Offer Conversion

Content only converts when it bridges from insight → problem → "I solve this" → call-to-action. Here's how to restructure content to drive revenue.

### Content Pillar Distribution

- **60% Problem-Aware Content:** Name the pain your ICP feels
- **30% Credibility Content:** Share proof and results
- **10% Offer Content:** Explicitly ask for the sale

### Problem-Aware Post Examples

- "Your churn problem doesn't start at renewal. It starts at onboarding."
- "The hidden cost of product-CS misalignment."
- "3 signs your CS team is a cost center, not a revenue engine."
- "Your NRR is below 100% — here's what's actually broken."

### Credibility Post Examples

- The Imperva transformation story (+168% ACV)
- "How I turned a one-time revenue org into a subscription model"
- "Lessons from building a $65M services portfolio"

### Offer Post Examples (1x per week)

- "I'm taking on 2 new diagnostic clients this quarter. DM me 'REVENUE' if your NRR is below 100%."
- "In 8 weeks, I can show you exactly where your customer lifecycle is leaking revenue. DM to learn more."
- "If your CS team is stuck in support mode, I can help you turn it into a revenue engine. Let's talk."
