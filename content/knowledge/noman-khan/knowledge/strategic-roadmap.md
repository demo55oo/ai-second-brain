# 90-Day Strategic Roadmap — From Engagement to Revenue

**Prepared for:** Noman Khan — Customer Success & Services Operating Model Strategist
**Timeframe:** January – March 2026
**Phases:** Month 1 — Foundation · Month 2 — Activation · Month 3 — Conversion

---

## Executive Summary

### The Problem We're Solving

You've been consistently posting on LinkedIn and building visibility, but it hasn't translated into meaningful, repeatable income. The gap between engagement and revenue has become frustrating.

### The Root Cause (From Content Diagnosis)

1. **Positioning Confusion** — Old "Franken-positioning" attracted no specific buyer.
2. **Wrong Audience** — Leadership content attracted peers, not buyers with budgets.
3. **No Commercial Intent** — Zero content bridges from insight to call-to-action.
4. **No Packaged Offer** — Nothing for buyers to say "yes" to.

### The 90-Day Solution

This roadmap addresses your three stated goals:

- **Brand Clarity** — Collapse background into a single, market-clear positioning.
- **Monetization** — Launch Revenue Alignment Diagnostic ($25K–$65K).
- **Content → Revenue** — Transform content from engagement-only to conversion-focused.

### 90-Day Success Metrics

- **Target:** 1–2 diagnostic clients = $50K–$100K revenue
- 500–1,000 targeted outreach connections
- 6 lead magnets published (2 per month)
- 10–15 qualified discovery calls booked

## The 5 Strategic Pillars

Everything in this roadmap is organized around five pillars that work together to convert engagement into revenue:

| Pillar | Focus | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Pillar 1** | One Clear Offer | Revenue Alignment Diagnostic ($25K–$65K) — packaged offer buyers can say "yes" to |
| **Pillar 2** | Targeted Outreach | 500–1,000 ICP connections via N8N and PhantomBuster automation + warm DM engagement |

## Pillar 1: The Offer Structure

### The Two-Tier Offer Funnel

- **Entry Point:** Free Revenue Leak Assessment (30-minute diagnostic call)
- **Core Offer:** Revenue Alignment Diagnostic ($25,000 – $65,000)

### Entry Point: Free Revenue Leak Assessment

**Purpose:** Qualify prospects, demonstrate expertise, create urgency.

- 30-minute Zoom call with CRO/CCO/VP CS
- Ask 5–7 diagnostic questions about their customer lifecycle
- Identify 2–3 visible gaps or misalignments
- Bridge to full diagnostic: *"What I've shared is the tip of the iceberg..."*

### Core Offer: Revenue Alignment Diagnostic

- **Duration:** 8 weeks (4 phases)
- **Deliverables:** Diagnostic report, operating model blueprint, roadmap, quick wins
- **Pricing:** Essentials ($25K) | Transformation ($40K) | Acceleration ($65K)
- **Guarantee:** 50% refund after Phase 2 if insights aren't valuable

## Pillar 2: Targeted Outreach

**Goal:** Connect with 500–1,000 people in your ICP over 90 days through systematic automation + personal engagement.

### ICP Targeting Criteria

- **Titles:** CRO, CCO, VP Customer Success, VP Post-Sales, CEO/Founder
- **Industries:** SaaS, Cybersecurity, Enterprise Technology
- **Company Size:** $10M–$500M ARR, 50–2,000 employees
- **Geography:** North America, UK/EU, Australia

### N8N and PhantomBuster Automation

- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator Search** — Build lists of ICP matches
- **Auto-Connect with Note** — 20–30 connection requests/day
- **Auto-Profile View** — Warm prospects before connecting
- **CRM Tracking** — Log all connections in a spreadsheet

### DM Engagement Sequence

| Timing | Message |
|--------|---------|
| Day 1 | "Thanks for connecting! I noticed you're leading CS at [Company]. How's retention looking heading into Q1?" |
| Day 3–5 | Share a relevant lead magnet. "Thought you might find this useful—it's a framework I used at Imperva." |
| Day 7–10 | "Would a quick 20-minute call help diagnose where your lifecycle might be leaking revenue?" |

## Pillar 4: Conversion Content Strategy

**Goal:** Shift from "engagement content" to "conversion content" with commercial intent.

### Content Distribution Formula

- **60% Problem-Aware**
- **30% Credibility/Proof**
- **10% Direct Offer**

### Problem-Aware Content (60%)

- "Your churn problem doesn't start at renewal. It starts at onboarding."
- "3 signs your CS team is a cost center, not a revenue engine."
- "Your NRR is below 100% here's what's actually broken."

### Credibility/Proof Content (30%)

- The Imperva transformation story (+168% ACV)
- "Lessons from building a $65M services portfolio"

### Direct Offer Content (10%)

- "I'm taking on 2 new diagnostic clients. DM me 'REVENUE' if your NRR is below 100%."
- "In 8 weeks, I can show you where your lifecycle is leaking revenue."

### Monthly Content Requirements

- 2 Monthly Strategy Calls
- 15–20 researched topics (provided by the team with research)
- 4 personal story topics (Noman provides, tied to expertise)
- Voice DNA document (ensures content sounds like Noman)
- CTA in every post ("DM me 'REVENUE'" or "Comment 'CHECKLIST'")

## Week-by-Week Execution Plan

### Month 1: Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

**Week 1–2: Brand Refresh**
- Update LinkedIn profile (headline, about, featured)
- Publish pinned post: "This is what I do and who I help."
- Set up N8N and PhantomBuster automation

**Week 3–4: Launch Foundation**
- Publish first 2 lead magnets (Churn Audit + NRR Calculator)
- Launch a simple landing page with Calendly
- Begin daily content posting (problem-aware focus)
- Start DM engagement with new connections

### Month 2: Activation (Weeks 5–8)

**Week 5–6: Scale Outreach**
- Reach 250+ new ICP connections
- Publish lead magnets 3–4
- Book first 3–5 Revenue Leak Assessment calls
- Publish the Imperva case study post

**Week 7–8: First Conversions**
- Send first 2–3 diagnostic proposals
- Host or speak at 1 event/roundtable
- **TARGET:** Close first diagnostic client ($25K–$40K)

### Month 3: Conversion (Weeks 9–12)

**Week 9–10: Scale Conversions**
- Reach 500+ total ICP connections
- Publish lead magnets 5–6
- Book 5–8 total assessment calls

**Week 11–12: Close + Optimize**
- **TARGET:** Close second diagnostic client ($40K–$65K)
- Review 90-day metrics
- Plan Q2 roadmap based on learnings
