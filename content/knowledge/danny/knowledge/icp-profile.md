ICP PERSONA SUMMARY
The Purely Personal ideal client is a solo founder or small business owner with real expertise and proven results  but zero visibility online. They are typically 28–50 years old, running a service-based or knowledge-based business generating $30K–$500K annually. They understand that AI and LinkedIn matter but have never built a consistent, strategic system that converts. They have tried posting on their own, experimented with AI tools, and possibly hired a generic agency — all without sustainable results. They are not looking for more content ideas or templates. They want an AI system that sounds like them, a personal brand that generates inbound, and a community of founders who are building the same way. They make decisions quickly once trust is established — and that trust is built through Daniel's story, real client results, and the free value they receive through the newsletter and AI Founder Circle before they ever pay.

STRATEGIC CONTENT DIRECTION
Daniel's content should speak directly to the moment before the decision — the solo founder who is watching others grow on LinkedIn and wondering why it isn't working for them. Every post should do one of three things: mirror their exact frustration back at them, show proof that the AI system works through specific founder stories, or teach one piece of the system that makes them think "I need the rest of this." The tone stays warm, direct, and story-driven. The positioning stays clear: Daniel is not another LinkedIn coach or AI tool vendor, he is the founder who built his own brand from nothing, trained 1,000+ founders to do the same, and now teaches solo founders and small business owners to use AI to grow their brand and scale their business without needing a technical background or a marketing team.
