MESSAGING DO'S AND DON'TS
Do:
	•	Lead with the founder's pain, they feel seen before we ask for anything
	•	Use specific numbers, 1.9M impressions, 1,000+ founders, 6x Top-Rated
	•	Tell real stories, Mehsum, Raila, cohort graduates
	•	Make AI feel accessible "if you can type, you can run it"
	•	Emphasize the system over the hustle
	•	Position Daniel's story as proof, an immigrant who built from nothing
	•	Make the community feel like belonging, not just access
Don't:
	•	Lead with credentials before establishing the reader's pain
	•	Use jargon, no "synergy," "leverage points," "omnichannel."
	•	Overpromise no "6 figures in 30 days."
	•	Make AI sound like it does everything, the founder's voice is central
	•	Sound corporate,  this is a founder talking to founders
	•	Use "not because... but because" or other robotic contrast patterns
	•	Make it about LinkedIn only , the positioning is broader now
