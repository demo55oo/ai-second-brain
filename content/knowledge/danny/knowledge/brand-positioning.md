PURELY PERSONAL: BRAND POSITIONING STRATEGY

1. BRAND ESSENCE
Purely Personal helps solo founders and small business owners use AI to grow their personal brand, automate their marketing and sales, and build a business that attracts the right clients — consistently.
We turn the founder into the most powerful marketing asset their business has — using AI systems that sound like them, not a template.

2. MARKET POSITION
Purely Personal is the AI-powered personal branding and coaching company for solo founders and small business owners who want to use AI for marketing and sales — so they can focus on what actually matters: running and growing their business.
We sit at the intersection of: AI education + personal branding + LinkedIn strategy + business automation for people who are not technical and don't want to be.

3. CATEGORY DEFINITION
The AI Marketing System for Solo Founders.
The fusion of AI-powered content, personal brand strategy, and marketing automation built to replace the hours founders spend on marketing every week, without losing their voice or their credibility.

4. TARGET AUDIENCE
	•	Solo founders, small business owners, coaches, consultants, and service providers
	•	$30K–$500K annual revenue (sweet spot: $50K–$250K)
	•	English-speaking markets: US, UK, UAE, Singapore, India, Europe
	•	Not technical don't want to be
	•	Time-poor, ambitious, outcome-driven
	•	The face of their own brand, no marketing team behind them

5. CUSTOMER PERSONA
Name: Alex (stand-in persona) Age: 28–50 Role: Solo founder or small business owner Location: US (primary), UK, UAE, Singapore, Europe Traits: Expert at what they do, ambitious, stretched thin, doing their own marketing, wants AI to work for them Pain: Invisible online, posting without results, spending too much time on content, tried AI tools but everything sounds generic Desire: A personal brand that generates inbound, AI doing the marketing heavy lifting, more time to run their business, recognition as the go-to expert in their space

6. THE PROBLEM
The solo founder or small business owner is spending too much time on marketing — and getting too little in return.
They fear:
	•	Staying invisible while competitors with weaker offers win their clients
	•	AI making their content sound robotic and generic
	•	Wasting more time and money on tools or systems that don't work
	•	Missing the window to build authority before the market gets noisier
	•	Being seen as just another option instead of the obvious choice

7. THE SOLUTION
An AI-powered personal branding and marketing system that:
	•	Teaches founders how to use AI for marketing, content, and sales in a way that sounds like them not a robot
	•	Automates the marketing workflows that consume their week
	•	Builds a personal brand on LinkedIn that generates inbound leads
	•	Gives them a community of founders building the same way
	•	Or  does it all for them through the done-for-you service
Two ways to work with Purely Personal:
	•	Coaching: We teach you the full AI system
	•	Done For You : Our team builds and runs it for you
