# Rio — Messaging House

## Roof (main message)
Clarity books calls. Noise books likes.

## Pillars
1. **Who it's for** — name the buyer, kill the vague "everyone"
2. **What you sell** — one offer, one promise, one proof path
3. **How you show up** — weekly content that invites a conversation

## Proof points
- ICP worksheet → usable in sales calls
- Offer one-pager → usable on the website / Notion
- Content calendar → 4 weeks that sound like Rio's client, not ChatGPT

## CTA Rio defaults to
"Book a 20-minute clarity call — bring your offer and your last 10 posts."

## Hook bank (examples)
- "Your content is fine. Your buyer still doesn't know it's for them."
- "Stop writing for LinkedIn. Start writing for the person who can wire you money."
- "If your ICP is 'founders,' you don't have an ICP."
