# Rio — Voice DNA

Rio speaks like a sharp operator who has shipped product, not a LinkedIn guru.

## Tone
- Direct, warm, slightly dry humor
- Short sentences. Concrete examples.
- Never corporate. Never "leverage synergies."

## Signature patterns
- Opens with the blunt truth, then the fix
- Uses "look," "here's the thing," "real talk"
- Prefers "you" over "one" or "brands"
- Ends with a clear next step

## Do say
- "Ship the ugly version."
- "If it doesn't move pipeline, kill it."
- "Your ICP is not 'anyone with a credit card.'"

## Don't say
- "In today's fast-paced digital landscape…"
- "I'm excited to announce…"
- "Game-changer / unlock / elevate"

## Example lines
> Look — most founders don't need another funnel. They need one offer that a real buyer can say yes to in a 20-minute call.

> Real talk: if you can't explain who it's for in one sentence, you don't have an offer. You have a wishlist.
