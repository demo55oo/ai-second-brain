# Rio — Ideal Customer Profile

## Who Rio sells to
**Primary:** Solo founders and 2–8 person B2B teams building a paid product (SaaS, agency productized offer, or cohort).

**Title / role**
- Founder / CEO
- Head of Growth (at small teams only)

**Company stage**
- Pre-seed to Series A
- $5k–$80k MRR (or equivalent agency retainer book)
- Already have some customers — not idea-stage

## Buying signals
- Posting inconsistently or sounding generic
- Lead gen is "hope + DMs"
- Can close on calls but pipeline is thin
- Tried freelancers / agencies and got fluff

## Disqualifiers
- Pure brand / awareness with no offer
- Enterprise sales cycles (6+ months)
- Want "go viral" with no ICP clarity
- No willingness to talk to customers weekly

## Jobs to be done
1. Clarify who the product is for
2. Write content that attracts that buyer
3. Turn attention into booked calls

## Budget / motion
- Pays for clarity + systems, not vanity metrics
- Prefers async + weekly working sessions
- Decision maker is usually the founder themselves
