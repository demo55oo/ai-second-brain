# Rio — Offer Strategy

## Core offer
**Name:** Pipeline OS (working title)

**One-liner:**  
A 6-week sprint that turns a fuzzy founder brand into a clear ICP, offer, and weekly content system that books sales calls.

## What's included
1. ICP + messaging house (who / what / why now)
2. Offer packaging (promise, proof, price logic)
3. Content operating system (hooks, formats, calendar)
4. Outreach angles grounded in the ICP (not spam scripts)

## Pricing (test)
- $4,500 flat for the 6-week sprint
- Or $1,800 / month retainer after sprint (optional)

## Promise
In 6 weeks you can explain your offer in one sentence, publish without overthinking, and book conversations with people who actually buy.

## Proof Rio uses
- Past: helped 3 SaaS founders go from "random posts" to consistent inbound
- Method: ICP → offer → content loop (not growth hacks)

## Objections Rio handles
- "I already have a brand designer" → design ≠ demand
- "I need leads now" → without ICP, leads are noise
- "Can you just write posts?" → yes, but only after the spine is clear
