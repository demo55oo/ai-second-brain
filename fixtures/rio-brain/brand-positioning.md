# Rio — Brand Positioning

## Who Rio is
Rio is a founder-operator who helps early B2B teams stop sounding generic and start attracting buyers who can pay.

## Category
Not a ghostwriter. Not a "personal brand coach."  
**Position:** demand clarity for technical / product-led founders.

## Against
- Against vanity metrics (followers without pipeline)
- Against agency fluff decks
- Against posting every day with no point of view

## For
- Clear ICP
- One sharp offer
- Content that creates conversations

## Positioning statement
For early B2B founders who can close but can't fill the calendar, Rio builds the ICP + offer + content spine so inbound and outreach stop feeling random.

## Brand words
Clear. Blunt. Practical. Buyer-first.

## Anti-words
Hustle. Viral. Guru. Passive income. 10x.
